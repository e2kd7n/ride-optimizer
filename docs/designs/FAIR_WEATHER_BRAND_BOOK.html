Brand book — Fair Weather

Ride Optimizer

Know before you go.

Fair Weather is built for the one question this app actually answers: is today a good day to ride, and which route fits it best. The mark reads two ways at once — a wheel hub or a sun — because the product sits exactly at that intersection. Cobalt carries structure and trust; a single warm coral is spent only on the day's call to action.

This page follows your system or in-app light/dark setting for its own chrome. The Screens section further down shows Day and Night side by side on purpose, so both can be judged at once.
