Step 1 of 4 — Create a Strava API Application

Ride Optimizer needs a Strava API application to read your activity data. This is a one-time setup.

  1. Go to strava.com/settings/api and sign in.
  2. Click Create & Manage Your App.
  3. Fill in any app name (e.g. My Ride Optimizer) and set the Authorization Callback Domain to localhost.
  4. Submit the form. Your Client ID and Client Secret will appear at the top of the page.
Your credentials are stored only on this device and are never sent to any third party.
